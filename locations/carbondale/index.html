Comprehensive Family Law Services in the Roaring Fork Valley

From our Carbondale office, Neiley Law serves clients throughout the Roaring Fork Valley, including Aspen, Basalt, Snowmass Village, Glenwood Springs, and surrounding communities.

High-Net-Worth Family Law

Our family law attorneys specialize in sophisticated domestic relations cases, including:

  • High-Asset Divorce: Handling complex divorces involving luxury real estate, business interests, investment portfolios, and substantial assets with the sophisticated expertise these cases demand.
  • Complex Property Division: Dividing marital estates that include Roaring Fork Valley properties, vacation homes, ski-in/ski-out real estate, commercial holdings, and investment properties.
  • Business Valuation: Working with forensic accountants to accurately value privately-held businesses, professional practices, and investment interests.
  • Executive Compensation: Addressing stock options, deferred compensation, bonuses, and complex income structures common among Valley professionals.
  • Prenuptial and Postnuptial Agreements: Drafting sophisticated marital agreements to protect substantial assets and family wealth.
  • Child Custody: Creating parenting plans that address private schools, international travel, multiple residences, and high-profile family considerations.
  • Spousal Maintenance: Negotiating and litigating appropriate maintenance given high-income marriages and significant lifestyle considerations.
  • Property Protection: Safeguarding inherited wealth, trust interests, and separate property in divorce proceedings.

Parenting and Custody Matters

We represent parents in every type of parenting dispute, including allocation of parental responsibilities, parenting time, decision-making, relocation, and modifications of existing orders. Our approach is grounded in the best interests of the children while protecting our clients' relationships and rights.

Civil Protection Orders and Family Law Appeals

We represent both petitioners and respondents in civil protection order proceedings, and we handle appeals of family law judgments before the Colorado Court of Appeals.
