Comprehensive Family Law Services in Rifle

At our Rifle office, Neiley Law offers a full range of family law services designed to meet the diverse needs of Garfield County residents:

  • Divorce and Legal Separation: Guiding clients through the divorce process with attention to child welfare, fair property division, and financial stability.
  • Child Custody and Parenting Time: Creating practical parenting plans that work with work schedules, school calendars, and the realities of Western Slope life.
  • Child Support: Ensuring accurate child support calculations under Colorado guidelines, including modifications when circumstances change.
  • Spousal Support: Advocating for fair spousal maintenance during and after marriage dissolution.
  • Property Division: Fairly dividing marital property, including homes, vehicles, retirement accounts, and personal property.
  • High-Asset Divorce: Handling complex divorces involving real estate, business interests, and substantial marital estates.
  • Modifications: Updating custody, support, and parenting time orders when life circumstances change, such as job loss, relocation, or remarriage.
  • Civil Protection Orders: Representing petitioners and respondents in protection order proceedings.
  • Prenuptial and Postnuptial Agreements: Drafting and negotiating marital agreements to protect separate property and family wealth.
  • Family Law Appeals: Appellate representation before the Colorado Court of Appeals.
